MARC JACOBS: DAISY
1957
Charmingly simple with a signature quality, Daisy Marc Jacobs transports you to a place that optimistic, beautiful and pure. The top of the fragrance is infused with succulent wild berries and soft white violets. At the heart, velvety jasmine warms to a dreamy drydown of sensual sandalwood, for a romantic, lingering finish. Irresistibly feminine, Marc Jacobs Daisy is the captivating creation of Master Perfumer Alberto Morillas.
